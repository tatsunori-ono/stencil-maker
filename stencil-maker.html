Layer masks are black-on-white cutouts of just that one band's regions — print one per layer if you're cutting a multi-layer stencil. The dark and mid bands are cut from the photo's shadow structure, not from raw color, so shapes stay chunky and cuttable. "Subject only" removes the background before any of this runs — nothing is uploaded anywhere, the AI model just has to download once. Auto-hue is a starting guess (skin and warm wood dominate most photos and throw it off); use the contact sheet or the hue slider to pick what actually looks right.
