Layer masks are black-on-white cutouts of just that one color's regions — print one per layer if you're cutting a multi-layer stencil, or use the combined PNG if you're cutting all colors from a single sheet with different techniques (paint fill, etc). Higher despeckle values remove small floating islands and thin slivers, which are the hardest part to cut cleanly. "Subject only" runs an AI model fully in your browser to cut out the background automatically — nothing is uploaded anywhere; the model just has to download once.
